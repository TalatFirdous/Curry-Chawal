Curry Chawal is Ready

Ingredients

  • chana daal
  • masoor daal
  • chawal
  • termeric powder
  • red chili powder
  • curry patta
  • onion paste
  • ginger and garlic paste
  • salt
  • jeera
  • tej patta
  • curd

Instructions

Make the Kadhi:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together yogurt, gram flour, green chilies, turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt until smooth.
  2. Heat oil in a kadai. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add cumin seeds and asafoetida.
  3. Gradually pour the yogurt mixture into the kadai, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  4. Cook on low-medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the kadhi thickens and comes to a boil.
  5. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Garnish with coriander leaves.

    6. Make the Pakoras (optional):

  6. In a bowl, mix gram flour, red chili powder, turmeric powder, salt, and water to form a batter.
  7. Add chopped onion (optional) to the batter.
  8. Heat oil in a deep pan.
  9. Drop spoonfuls of batter into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy.
  10. Drain on paper towels.

    11. Make the Rice:

  11. Wash the rice thoroughly and soak for 30 minutes.
  12. Drain the rice and cook it in a pressure cooker with 4 cups of water for 2-3 whistles.
  13. Heat ghee in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  14. Add the cooked rice and fluff it up gently.

    15. Enjoy your homemade Kadhi Chawal!
